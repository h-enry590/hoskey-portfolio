Behind the Lens

Beyond the camera: a journey of faith, culture, and purpose.

🌍

Nationality

I am proudly Ghanaian. Born and raised in Walewale in the Northern Region, my identity is deeply rooted in the culture, traditions, and warmth of my people. Every story I tell carries a piece of Ghana with it.

🙏

My Faith Journey

I was born into a Traditionalist family, deeply connected to the ancestral customs and spiritual practices of my people. Later in life, I converted to Islam and walked that path for a season. But my search for truth did not end there.

Today, I am a proud Christian. My conversion to Christianity was not just a change of religion: it was a transformation of the heart. I found in Christ the peace, purpose, and identity I had been searching for. This is why I carry both a traditional name (Ziblim) and a Christian name (James): they represent the full story of who I am — a man shaped by heritage, renewed by faith.

🎭

My Lifestyle

I live a life of purpose, creativity, and discipline. My days are spent behind the camera, in the editing suite, or on location bringing stories to life. But beyond the work, I am a man who values connection: with people, with culture, and with God.

I believe in living simply so that others may simply live. I am grounded in my community, faithful in my relationships, and driven by a vision to see African stories told with the dignity and excellence they deserve. When I am not working, you will find me reading, mentoring young creators, or spending time with family in Walewale.
